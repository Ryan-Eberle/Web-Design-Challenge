Latitude vs. Wind Speed

There could be a slight correlation in wind speed at higher latitudes for our data set. The trend is weak, but having a few outlier high wind speed data points would pull the trend up. This low correlation would be expected unless the data cities were in close geographic location to each other and there was a not normal atmospheric event(e.g. hurricane)
